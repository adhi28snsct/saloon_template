# AGENT.md

## 📌 Product Overview

This is a **Salon Booking & Customer Management SaaS Platform** designed to help service-based businesses manage bookings, customers, and retention in one place.

It includes:

* Public booking system (no login required)
* Admin dashboard for business management
* Customer tracking and follow-up automation

---

## 🎯 Problem It Solves

Most salons and small service businesses currently manage operations using:

* WhatsApp chats
* Phone calls
* Notebooks

This results in:

* Missed or double bookings
* Poor customer tracking
* Forgotten follow-ups
* Loss of repeat customers
* No insights into business performance

---

## 💡 Solution

This system provides a **complete booking + CRM + retention platform**:

### 🌐 Customer Side (Public Booking)

* Customers can book appointments without login
* Access via link (Instagram bio, WhatsApp, ads)
* View services and choose time slots
* Simple and fast booking experience

### 🔐 Admin Side (Dashboard)

* Manage all bookings in one place
* Track customer data and history
* Monitor follow-ups and retention
* View business analytics

---

## 🧩 Core Features

### 1. Public Booking System (NEW 🔥)

* Unique booking link per business (`/salon/[slug]`)
* Service selection UI
* Date & time slot booking
* No login required
* Direct sync with admin dashboard

---

### 2. Dashboard (Admin)

* Overview of daily operations
* Revenue tracking
* Appointment insights
* Business performance metrics

---

### 3. Appointments Management

* Real-time booking updates
* Status tracking (Booked / Confirmed / Completed)
* Manual + automatic booking support
* Prevent double bookings

---

### 4. Customer Management (CRM)

* Auto-create customers from bookings
* Track visit history
* Store notes and preferences
* Monitor total spending and visits

---

### 5. Follow-up System (Key Feature 🔥)

* Track pending follow-ups
* Highlight "Due Today" actions
* Manual + auto follow-up creation
* Improve repeat customer rate

---

### 6. Lead Source Tracking (NEW)

* Track where bookings come from:

  * Website
  * Instagram
  * WhatsApp
  * Ads
* Helps businesses understand customer acquisition

---
